CaptionAI Case Study

Project Overview

CaptionAI is an AI powered caption generator that improves the work flow of Cloud Campaign customers which range from large agencies to freelancers. The product has two forms, one as a standalone product and two as an integrated feature within Cloud Campaign's agency-friendly social media management platform. Given the two forms, the purpose, the considerations, and the pain points varied from the beginning.

Teams

  • Product

Role

Sole UX/UI Designer

Key Points of Consideration

  • How to handle limited space in the location of implementation
  • How to integrate and introduce the implementation without disrupting the existing work flows of our users
  • How to add value and satisfaction without hindering user experience

Yes

  • Keep documentation up to date and accessible to all necessary parties

Research

Ideation

For this project, I carried out a design sprint to the best of my abilities in order to gather more insight and heighten collaboration. As the only designer, there was often a need for initiating contact for brainstorming and ideating. I would like to note, this was my first time running a design sprint and given all that could have gone better, it truly was better than not having the design sprint.

Wireframes and Iterations

Visual Design

For visual design, when creating the high fidelity wireframes, I stayed consistent with the established brand color scheme. As for established patterns, decisions were made on a case by case basis. Should we keep the existing pattern or should we introduce a new one and implement backwards? I value consistency, but I am an advocate for new patterns when and where necessary, given they provide a smoother and more inclusive experience. This is also taking into consideration the time and resources available to implement the changes that follow a new pattern.

Result and Reflection

This new feature has been successfully released and is being utilized by a noticeable majority of Cloud Campaign customers. With that, there has been an inflow of postive reviews with regards to the design and the functionality of the feature. Personally, this is one of my proudest projects at Cloud Campaign as I stepped out of my comfort zone and reached out for increased collaboration. This in turn led to a fuller list of design requirements, better understanding of the technical feasability, and clearer insight into user needs. This allowed for easier navigation when faced with scope creep and unforseen adjustments.
